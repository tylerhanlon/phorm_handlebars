Small Group Phormula

Small group training is a unique, new concept that has been gaining popularity. Here at Phorm, we limit our group size to a mazimum of 4 people. We call our program "Phormula 4."

You will still get the same private experience with a certified trainer, but you also get the added motivation by working out with other members. Our trainers push our groups to set new goals to help you acheive the body you want. A little friendly competition never hurts!
